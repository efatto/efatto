Professionally Designed Invoice/P.O/R.F.Q/S.O/S.Q/Delivery Note/Picking List - Available for Odoo 8.0 & 9.0

Take your business to the next level by using professionally designed reports templates for your Invoices, Sales Orders, Quotations, RFQs, Purchase Orders, Delivery Notes and Picking Lists. Ensure that you use your company theme colors, high resolution logos and uniform design in all your business reports. This Module will install Five customizable templates for every document mentioned above. You will be able to select one among the five templates for each document/report and customize it with a nice large company logo, Company colors, Color of the text and even the background color of the invoice lines or sales order lines depending on which document you are customizing

Main Features

  1. Compatible with Odoo 9.0 Enterprise, 9.0 Community and Odoo 8.0
  2. Seven (7) Types of reports installed : Invoice, Purchase Order, RFQ, Sales Order, Quotation, Delivery Note and Picking List
  3. Five (5) Templates to choose from per every Report: Modern Template, Classic Template, Retro Template, Odoo Template and TVA Template
  4. You can upload and use larger and high resolution company logos that are much more visible and correctly positioned in the document
  5. Set the theme colors for your report to match your company colors or any other set of colors of your choice
  6. You can set the text color for your company name in the report separately from other text to be distinctly visible
  7. You can set the text color for your customer name or vendor name in the report separately from other text to be distinctly visible
  8. You can set the Background Color for odd and even lines (i.e invoice or quotation lines) in all the reports to make it more readable
  9. Choose the Font-Family of your report by specifying the name of the font. Also set the default Font-Family of all your reports in the company profile
  10. Adjust the Font-size of the header, body anf footer to your preferred size per report. Also set the default font-sizes for all reports in the Company profile
  11. All Lines in the reports have numbering (i.e quotation lines, order lines)
  12. You will be able to configure default colors and theme settings for all reports (in the company form) and also custom settings per report in the respective report form (e.g Invoice or sales order form)
  13. If you want to further customize these templates, then is much possible since they are Qweb type hence customizable...We can also assit you to add more customizations to your report

Company Form: Default Report Settings for all reports

You can customize the 'DEFAULT' theme colors, font-family, font-size, and logo for each type of your report by changing the report settings found in the company profile. You can also customize colors, font-family, font-size and logo for specific report by changing the report settings in the respective report form. For example, if you want to customize colors, font, font-size and logo for a specific invoice for a certain customer, you can do so in the invoice form.This will override the default report settings configured in the company profile form
